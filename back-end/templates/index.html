About

Fatigue and microsleep at the wheel are often the cause of serious accidents. However, the initial signs of fatigue can be detected before a critical situation arises. The Safe drive drowsiness detection can do this by monitoring eye movements and alerting the driver with an alarm.

Safety doesn't happen by accident.

  • Drowsiness detection through object detection
  • Alerting by alarm
  • Driver monitoring

Operation principle of driver drowsiness detection

The driver drowsiness detection is based on an algorithm, which begins recording the driver’s movements the moment the trip begins. Eyes are detected from the extracted frames and passed to the model which recognizes the changes in eye movements of the driver. Whenever the driver is asleep, an alarm is triggered and he quickly gets alert and keeps the vehicle on track.
